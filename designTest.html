Welcome Page

The University of Waterloo Philosophy Society is mandated to serve the philosophic interests of the community of undergraduate students at the University of Waterloo. More details about our operating structure can be found in our Constitution. Those reasponsible for implementing the clubs mandate our the Executive. Below you will find a brief bit of information about the clubs current goals, and this terms operating budget.

Goals

  • Establish a relationship with the various Philosophic groups on campus and within the KW community
  • Create a well managed society which will persist into the future
  • Plan events for the Philosophy students at the University of Waterloo
